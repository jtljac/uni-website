References:

With the development of a new console generation every few years, there are exclusive games and products (accessories) that both PlayStation and Xbox include. Depending largely on this, both brands strike up deals with game developing companies and other giant technology companies to produce state of the art products. For example, for the first time in PlayStation history, Sony developed the PS VR in 2016, which is a virtual reality set for the PlayStation 4; no other major gaming console besides PlayStation had an accessory like this and this created a lot of buzz amongst its competitors. Sony has released games which are PS VR exclusives such as Resident Evil 7 and Gran Turismo Sport, this was a huge move as both games hold high popularity ratings. This led to a partnership between Microsoft and Oculus (a company which specialises in virtual reality) to create the oculus rift which is a virtual reality headset that is compatible with the Xbox one, Xbox one s and Xbox one r. The PS VR is the market leader in the virtual reality hardware business, occupying 30% of the total market share as of August 2019, the Oculus rift comes in second place with 22%. This demonstrates the importance of having exclusives, especially if it is the first of that kind of technology in its industry because it allows large companies to gain the upper hand such as Sony for the PlayStation.
